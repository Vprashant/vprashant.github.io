About Prashant Verma

I am from Kanpur, India, and completed my schooling and engineering graduation at UIET, CSJM University Kanpur. After my computer science engineering degree, I pursued a master's in computer science in Bhopal, where I developed a strong interest in applied mathematics and data science. I worked as a researcher at IISER-KOL, focusing on computational biology and financial time series analysis. Later, I served as a senior research fellow at the India Agriculture Statistical Research Institute and the National Institute of Medical Statistics. Currently, I am a computer vision specialist at L&T Tech Services. I previously worked for HCL for over 3 years, specializing in computer vision, data science, and AI backend applications using Flask, Django, and Python.
My research is centered on computer vision, Generative AI, and NLP. I am actively working on cutting-edge GenAI algorithm-based product development and have founded GenAI product Eulerflow and Graph Database.
