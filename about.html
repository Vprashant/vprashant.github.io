About Prashant Verma

I hail from a city in Uttar Pradesh, Kanpur, and have done all my schooling and engineering graduation at UIET, CSJM University Kanpur. After graduating in computer science engineering, I moved to Bhopal for my master's in computer science. During my master's, I felt more inclined towards applied mathematics and data science. During my course, I found an opportunity at IISER-KOL as a researcher (computational biology and financial time series analysis) under the supervision of Dr. Sukant Khurana. After that, I also worked as a senior research fellow at the India Agriculture Statistical Research Institute and the National Institute of Medical Statistics.
I am currently a computer vision specialist for L&T Tech Services; previously, I worked for HCL for 3+ years in computer vision, data science, and building AI backend applications in Flask, Django, and Python. My research focuses on computer vision and AI solutions.
Apart from my technical profile, I love to visit historic sites; playing games and listening to music are my best pastimes.
